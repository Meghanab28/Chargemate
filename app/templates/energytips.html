Energy Saving Tips

Maximize Your EV's Efficiency

1. Drive Smoothly

Smooth driving can increase your EV's range by reducing energy consumption.

  • Gradually accelerate from stops.
  • Maintain a consistent speed.
  • Avoid unnecessary hard braking.
2. Monitor Tire Pressure

Proper tire pressure ensures efficiency and safety.

  • Check tire pressure monthly.
  • Follow manufacturer-recommended PSI levels.
3. Limit Air Conditioning Use

Using air conditioning drains your EV's battery. Optimize its usage:

  • Use eco-mode settings.
  • Open windows at low speeds.
4. Use Regenerative Braking

Regenerative braking recovers energy as you slow down:

  • Enable regenerative braking mode.
  • Coast to stops instead of braking hard.
